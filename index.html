Engagement Letter
Thank you for instructing Magna Money to act on behalf of you and your business. We take pride in offering a world class service and providing the best solution for our clients.

In order to do this, it is important to set out our understanding of the Commercial Finance services that you require us to perform for your business, and the respective fees we will charge you:-

The Services

You have asked us to source business finance for you. The service will consist of:

  • Receiving and reviewing financial information from you
  • Shortlisting relevant lenders from our lender panel
  • Packaging and submitting a lending proposal to each lender
  • Negotiating with each lender to ensure best offer
  • Reviewing and presenting a list of offers back to you
  • Assistance in the execution of the chosen facility

Fees

In accordance with normal professional practice, our fees are based upon the degree of responsibility, skill involved, and the time necessarily occupied on the work.

1. Upfront engagement fee (£)
2. Success fee (% of funding taken)
3. Success fee (fixed £ amount, if applicable)

Whichever lender you choose, we may also receive a commission from them (either a fixed fee or fixed % of the amount you borrow) and different lenders pay different rates.
